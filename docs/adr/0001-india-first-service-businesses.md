# ADR 0001 — India-first service businesses

## Context

Generic micro-business bookkeeping spans incompatible workflows. An opening wedge must reduce accounting variation.

## Decision

Target one-entity Indian service businesses, agencies, consultants, and small SaaS companies. Prioritize accountant-ready CSV/Excel, Zoho Books, and Tally workflows before broad international accounting integrations.

## Consequences

Inventory, payroll, complex FX, and multi-entity workflows are deferred. India-specific merchant narratives, Razorpay settlements, GST/TDS review routing, and accountant collaboration shape future phases.

## Revisit trigger

Three successful close cycles across the opening segment and evidence that an adjacent segment can be added without weakening controls.
